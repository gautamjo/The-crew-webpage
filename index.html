Scope of work

Here is our scope of work for this project: Ideation + concept design of the experience as VR involves domain specific scripting and storyboarding. Application development will primarily be for Gear VR and galaxy s7. Though, it could also be ported to google cardboard if you so choose. However, our recommendation would be Gear VR as it is the industry leading VR device for mobile at present. Besides that, configuring a controller for navigation and interaction will also lie within our scope of work. Pre-production scope of work would involve designing UI/UX, scripting narrations, VR specific camera angles and storyboarding.
We will also take care of audio post production for all stages from recording in a studio till the completion of a master recording. It will involve sound design, sound editing, audio mixing, and additional effects. We will also be remodelling and optimising 3d models for compatibility with the game engine. Furthermore, we will be retexturing, retouching and lighting pre-existing models for realistic look and feel. We will also be creating a VR logo animation for the splash screen. Now lets talk about the PC version. Here the our entire focus will be to create maximum immersion. Therefore, to acheive that we will have to go back in the app and redevelop a lot of things from scratch. New materials, Tracking system, Controllers, Lighting system (High Poly Models) and much more. Below are somethings you can expect in the PC version over the mobile version. Superior quality graphics. Smoother transitions. In game interactive controllers configured for Oculus/Vive devices. Fully Spacial tracking enabling users to stand, walk, lean or move around a space of 3MX3M.
